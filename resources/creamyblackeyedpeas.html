Creamy Blackeyed Peas

Ingredients

  • 3cans Black Eyed Peas
  • 8oz Cream Cheese
  • 1 large Onion
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 1tsp Minced Garlic
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Directions

  1. Saute Onion, Bell Pepper, and Minced Garlic until fragrent and onins are transulcent
  2. Add 3 cans of black eyed peas to the sauted veggies. Cook until bubbly
  3. Stir in cream cheese and simmer until thickened
