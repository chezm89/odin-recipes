Southern Fried Chicken

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • butter milk
  • 2tps hot sauce
  • 2 cups of cornmeal
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 bag chicken leg quarters
  • 2tps salt
  • 2tps pepper
  • 1tps garlic powder
  • 1tsp paprika
  • 1tps onion powder
  • cooking oil

Directions

  1. Cut chicken leg and thighs in half, then cut thighs and back in half
  2. mix eggs, butter milk, and hot sauce together
  3. pour over chicken and marinate for two hours or overnight
  4. mix cornmeal, flour, and seasonings together
  5. Batter chicken in the flour cornmeal mixture
  6. Heat oil to 375 and fry chicken for 12 to 15 minutes
