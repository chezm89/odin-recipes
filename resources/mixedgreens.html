Mixed Greens

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds collard greens and turnip greens, cleaned and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions

  1. Saute collard greens, turnip greens, onion, minced garlic, and seasonings
  2. Pour in the chicken broth and water
  3. Bring to a boil and then simer for 2 hours
